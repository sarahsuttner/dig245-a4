A Usability Report on the Davidson College Website

Conducted by Sarah Suttner, Stine Ornes, Emily Weld, and Nyjay Nelson

PROJECT OVERVIEW:

This project was designed to evaluate the usability of a website of our choice. My group chose to evaluate the Davidson College website. We created five questions in order to accomplish this task, and recorded the results below.

1

Find campus precautions for COVID 19

Accuracy: Not good, multiple errors. Information was found after many different approaches

CONCLUSION:

Our tester concluded that the page itself was not exceedingly helpful. He especially disliked that the coronavirus information was placed so obscurely. Overall, he considered the site a poorly designed one; someone who is not super technologically savvy would have struggled.
